All of our rings are custom fabricated according to the need of the client. Whether you need a custom ring for your gym, a movie shoot, or a charity event, Terry can fabricate it to fit any space. We rent or sell rings depending on what you need. We also transport and deliver.

Terry has worked with several production companies on small and large productions.
