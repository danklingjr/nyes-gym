Fighting Fitness

Nye's Gym is a boxing, kickboxing gym located in Lancaster, Pa. What makes Nye's Gym unique is its commitment to training its members like they are going to fight. Nye's Gym members are put through the same workouts as Nye's Gym fighters. Best buy $150 for 3 months. or 6 month agreement with a $20 sign up fee plus $55 per month.

Boxing

Boxing in Central Pennsylvania is a deep rooted tradition and legacy that Nye's Gym is proud to carry on. Boxing is a martial art, at Nye's Gym we train and instruct for fighting. Advancement in technique and fitness is our goal. The workouts in most chain gyms are lacking in advancement and designed only for beginners. Train to fight. Fight to win.

Kickboxing

Terry Nye is an experienced kickboxer. If you are thinking about trying kickboxing out then there is no better place to learn. It's not easy, you will have to push yourself, but you will learn the technique and gain the stamina needed to succeed and get in fighting shape.
